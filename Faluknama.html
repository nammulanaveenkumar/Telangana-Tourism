FALUKNAMA PALACE

Sitting at a startling elevation of 2,000 feet overlooking the city and known to be one of the best hotels in Hyderabad, this newly renovated resort is housed within an 1894 palace. Grand suites with marble floors, Colonial themes and designer furniture are offered to guests. They have two trendy restaurants serving Indian and Mediterranean meals. You can enjoy tea and hookah at their rooftop lounge and infinity pool.

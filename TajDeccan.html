TAJ DECCAN

Tucked amidst rolling hills and acres of well-manicured gardens, this luxury hotel is an abode for vacationers and corporates and is rightly called one of the best hotels in Hyderabad. Wood-lined suites with living rooms and workstations create a welcoming retreat. Some units have club quarters and refreshment lounges. Taj Deccan houses 2 Asian restaurants, one having an upscale atrium bar. An Ayurvedic spa and meeting facilities are also available on-site.
